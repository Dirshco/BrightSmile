📘 Privacy Policy

✅ Effective Date: May 18, 2026

1. Overview

Oral Health Tracker ("the App") is a health & fitness application designed to help users maintain good oral hygiene through reminders and educational content. This policy explains how we handle your information — we collect nothing.

2. Data Collection — Minimal by Design

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to any servers. The App operates entirely offline. All alarms, settings, and streak data are stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences.

What We DO NOT Collect:

  • Names, emails, phone numbers, or addresses
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, MAC address, etc.)
  • Usage analytics or crash logs
  • Any health or biometric data

What is stored locally (on your device only):

  • Alarm titles and times
  • Repeat day preferences
  • Consequence / streak counters

3. Permissions

PermissionPurpose
SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARMTo set precise alarm times for oral care reminders
POST_NOTIFICATIONS (Android 13+)To send you reminder notifications

These permissions are used only for core reminder functionality. No data from these permissions is collected or transmitted.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses no third-party services — no analytics SDKs, no ad networks, no crash reporting tools, no cloud storage.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. Because the App stores no data externally and transmits nothing, child data is not at risk.

6. Data Security

Since the App does not collect or transmit data, there is no data to intercept or breach. All local data is stored within the Android application sandbox, inaccessible to other apps without root access.

7. Your Rights

You have full control: Delete data by uninstalling the App. Modify data by editing or deleting alarms at any time.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

9. Contact Us

📧 Email: privacy@oralhealthtracker.com

This Privacy Policy applies to Oral Health Tracker version 1.0 and above.

ما يتم تخزينه محلياً (على جهازك فقط):
