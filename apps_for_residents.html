Essential Mobile Apps for New IM Residents

A curated collection of the most useful mobile applications for point-of-care reference, clinical decision support, drug information, and staying up-to-date with the latest evidence.

Pro Tips for New Residents

  • Check your hospital or residency program for institutional access to UpToDate, Epocrates, or DynaMed.
  • Start with UpToDate + MDCalc + Epocrates/Medscape as your core trio.
  • Enable offline mode where available for wards and call shifts.
  • Many apps offer free versions or trial periods — explore during orientation.
