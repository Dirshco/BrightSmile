The 2-Minute Habit

Regular cleaning prevents cavity buildup. Spend 30 seconds on each dental zone: Top-Left, Top-Right, Bottom-Left, and Bottom-Right for ultimate coverage!

🫧

Proper Denture Care

Always scrub plates or partials with non-abrasive denture paste, and let them soak overnight in cool water to preserve shape and avoid dry irritation sore-spots.

🚨 Warning: Clinical Warning Signals

If you encounter fast, heavy swelling that impedes breathing, severe facial swelling, or uncontrolled active bleeding that does not seal with direct compress pressure, immediately visit emergency care or contact your dental provider.
